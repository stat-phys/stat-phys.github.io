About Me

I received B.S. degree from Anhui University in 2009. Then, I studied the statistical physics as applied to complex networks and complex systems at University of Science and Technology of China, and received Ph.D. degree in 2014. From 2014 to 2016, I was a Post-doctoral Fellower in the Department of Modern Physics at University of Science and Technology of China. In the first half of 2016, I worked at Department of Applied Physics, The Hong Kong Polytechnic University as a Post-doctoral Fellower. From 2016 to 2021, I worked at School of Engineering Science, University of Science and Technology of China as a Research Associate Professor. Since 2022, I work at Department of Physics, Hefei University of Technology as a Professor of Physics.

Research Interests

  • Phase transition and critical phenomena
  • Percolation theory
  • Complex metworks
