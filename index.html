Welcome to Ming Li’s Group!

Our group conducts research on the Statistical Physics and Complex Systems, especially percolation transition, complex networks, and nonlinear dynamics. Combining statistical physics, computer simulations, and empirical methods, we investigate the organization mechanism and critical phenomena of various systems, based on which we aim to understrand the universal nature of natural and social complex systems.
